# Pocket AI School Foundation

- Shared across every model and hardware profile.
- Covers grades 1-12 in English and Greek.
- Includes deterministic arithmetic, fractions, percentages, statistics, equations, and geometry.
- Includes compact curriculum knowledge for mathematics, science, language, humanities, computing, and study skills.
- It is a broad learning aid, not an official national curriculum and not a substitute for a teacher or textbook.
