Unknown processors
------------------
Unknown model names are intentionally not treated as errors. PocketAI computes
a processor score from the measurable hardware and chooses a conservative
model. This also supports future processors without requiring a script update.

Classifier capacity selected after scanning
-------------------------------------------
Micro:    sub-1 GB or emergency/32-bit conditions
Lite:     approximately 1-1.5 GB and legacy low-end processors
Balanced: approximately 1.5-2.4 GB or slow entry processors
Standard: approximately 2.4-3.6 GB and Galaxy A12-class or better
Max:      approximately 3.6 GB+ when current free RAM and CPU score allow it

The thresholds are conservative runtime policies rather than manufacturer
compatibility guarantees. Available RAM and storage are checked dynamically,
so a phone may temporarily receive a smaller model when Android is under load.
