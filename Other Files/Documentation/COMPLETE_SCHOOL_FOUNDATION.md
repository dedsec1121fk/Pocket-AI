# Pocket AI Complete School Foundation

Version: 9.0 natural-complete-school

## Scope

- Grades 1 through 12
- 771 searchable curriculum topics
- 3,994 grade-adapted structured lessons
- English and Greek retrieval and response support
- Nine broad fields: mathematics; language and literature; science; history and humanities; geography; computing; health and life skills; arts and music; economics and civics

## Detailed lesson record

Each grade-linked lesson can provide:

1. learning goal
2. prerequisite knowledge
3. core explanation
4. teaching and understanding strategy
5. vocabulary
6. guided or worked example
7. common misconception
8. practice prompt
9. mastery check
10. related topics
11. local evidence

The SQLite database uses FTS5 for fast retrieval and opens read-only at runtime. The compact gzip pack keeps aliases, summaries, grade ranges, and grade maps available even if the database cannot be opened.

## Honest boundary

This is a broad cross-curriculum school foundation, not a verbatim copy of every national textbook, teacher plan, examination board, or continuously changing official syllabus. The assistant should state uncertainty and encourage checking important course-specific requirements.
